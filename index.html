About the Award

The ICONS GRAND BREAKERS AWARD is a global recognition platform created to honour individuals whose extraordinary contributions have shaped industries, communities, and humanity across all walks of life.

This award stands as a timeless memorial — celebrating excellence, courage, innovation, and impact that transcend generations.

Our Mission

To identify, honour, and preserve the legacy of icons whose groundbreaking achievements inspire the present and guide the future.
